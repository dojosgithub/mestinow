Privacy Policy for mestiNow

Last Updated: 2025-04-01

Thank you for using mestiNow! This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is handled within the mestiNow mobile application (the "App").

1. Consent

Upon the first launch of the App, we will clearly explain this Privacy Policy and ask for your consent to the collection and handling of your information as described herein.

2. Information We Collect and How We Use It

The mestiNow app is designed to help you track your Mestinon medication intake and monitor your Myasthenia Gravis (MG) symptoms. To provide these features, the App collects the following information that you voluntarily provide:

  • Medication Intake: Timestamps of when you log your Mestinon doses.
  • Symptom Logs: Information about your muscle strength, eye drooping, fatigue level, stomach discomfort, ptosis (drooping eyelids), vision problems, muscle weakness, neck symptoms, breathing difficulties, and walking issues, along with timestamps of when these symptoms are logged.
  • Optional Notes: Any additional details you choose to enter regarding your symptoms.
  • App Settings: Your preferences for notification sounds, vibration, reminder intervals, and dark/light mode.

This information is used solely within the App to:

  • Display the time since your last dose and the time remaining until your next recommended dose.
  • Provide you with timely medication reminders.
  • Allow you to track and review your medication adherence and symptom history.
  • Generate summary charts or timelines of your medication and symptom data.

3. Data Storage

All data you enter into the mestiNow app is stored locally on your mobile device. We do not collect or transmit your personal data to any external servers.

4. Data Security

We are committed to ensuring the security of your information. While your data is stored locally, we aim to ensure data encryption on your device where possible. We also strive for compliance with applicable healthcare privacy standards. However, as the data is stored locally, you are responsible for the security of your device.

5. Sharing Your Information

Since your data is stored locally on your device, we do not automatically share your information with any third parties. The App provides the ability to export your data, which you can then choose to share with your healthcare providers or others at your discretion.

6. Your Rights

As your data is stored locally, you have direct control over it. You can access, review, and delete your data directly within your device. If you uninstall the App, the data stored within it may be deleted depending on your device's operating system.

7. Accessibility

We are committed to making the App accessible. This includes providing options for font scaling and readability enhancements and clear, high-contrast visuals.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the App's privacy practices, please refer to the settings page or contact the app developer. You can also use the contact form available within the App.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated within the App.
